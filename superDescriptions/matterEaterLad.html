Matter Eater Lad

            Matter Eater Lad, secret identity Tensil Kem, is a native of the planet Bismoll, where microbes infected food sources and made them inedible. The native people of Bismoll evolved the ability to eat anything. According to wikipedia, "He appears rarely in Legion stories, as the writers struggled with the problem of how to make his power useful in a fight and was routinely written out..."
