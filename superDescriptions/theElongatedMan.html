The Elongated Man

            The Elongated Man, secret identity Randolph "Ralph" Dibney, is a member of the justice league with the ability to stretch himself out. He originally appeared in the comic book series The Flash in May of 1960. When he was a teenager, he was fascinated by the performances of contortionists. All of the famous contortionists he spoke with drank a soda called "Gingold." He learned chemistry and created a super-concentrated extract of the rare Yucatanian fruit, the Ginko, which is a primary ingredient of the body-bending beverage. This is how he acquired his elasticity.
